  Beagle

  Weight:   10-11kg
	
  Coat:   Short haired, hard coat of medium length
	
  Color:   Many colors
	
	The beagle is a breed of small hound that is similar in apearence to the much larger foxhound
	The beagle is a scent hound, developed primarily for hounting hare (beagling). With a greate sense of
	smell and superior tracking instinct, the beagle is employed as detection dog for prohibited agricultural
	imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent but single-minded. It is
	a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and lack of inherited health problems.
